Lasagna

Description

The cookie monster knew what was up.

The only thing better than a chocolate ship cookie, is a wookie-sized chocolate chip cookie.

Ingredients

  • Beef (1 lb)
  • Spaghetti Sauce (32 oz)
  • Cheeses(32 oz cottage cheese or r.icotta, 3 cups mozzarella, 1/2 cup parmesan)
  • Eggs (2)
  • Seasonings (2 tsp parsley, salt, pepper)
  • Lasagna Noodles (9)
  • Water (1/2 cup)

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the ground beef, then stir in the spaghetti sauce and simmer.
  2. Combine the cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella, eggs, half of the Parmesan, and seasonings.
  3. Assemble the lasagna according to the detailed recipe.
  4. Bake, covered, for 45 minutes.
  5. Uncover and continue baking for 10 minutes.
