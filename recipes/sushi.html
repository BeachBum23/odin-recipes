Philadelphia Roll Sushi

Description

You will see, that you looovve sushi.

Philadelphia rolls are about the only good thing associated with the city of philaelphia.

Ingredients

  • Smoked salmon (4 oz)
  • Cream Cheese (8 oz)
  • Dried Seaweed (2 sheets)
  • Avocado (1/2)
  • Sushi rice (1/2 cup cooked)

Steps

  1. Lay out a seaweed sheet and evenly spread rice across the bottom 1/3 of the seaweed sheet.
  2. Add 2 oz smoked salmon, 4 oz cream cheese, and a 1/4 avocado evenly across the rice.
  3. Use a sushi rolling mat to fold the seaweed sheet onto itself, enclosing the sushi ingreidents, and roll until cylindrical.
  4. Apply water to the top edge of the seaweed sheet to seal.
  5. Repeat with the other half of the ingredients.
